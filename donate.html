Donate to Code for DC

Thank you for your interest in helping to support Code for DC! Contributions of any size are helpful and welcome.

Donations to Code for DC are processed via Open Collective, a platform where communities can collect and disburse money transparently, to sustain and grow their projects. Code for DC is hosted by the Open Collective Foundation 501(c)(3), and all donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent of U.S. law.
