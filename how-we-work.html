How we work

Founded in 2012, Civic Tech DC is a non-partisan, non-political group of volunteer civic technologists working together to solve local issues and help people engage with the city.

What is "civic tech"?

"The use of technology for the public good.
…
Civic tech is tech that empowers people to have more agency and more of a role in the decisions that affect their lives, as well as tech that helps address public challenges and needs."
— Civic Tech Field Guide

Who we are
