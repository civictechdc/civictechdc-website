## Become a partner

Our best work starts when a partner brings a real problem to the table. If you see your organization in one of these, we'd like to hear from you.

<ul class="ctdc-partner-paths usa-list usa-list--unstyled">
  <li class="ctdc-partner-path">
    <h3 class="ctdc-partner-path__title">Organizations</h3>
    <p class="ctdc-partner-path__body">
      Our strongest partners have been local nonprofits and community-based
      organizations — digitizing paper processes, building new tools, and
      creating proofs-of-concept for civic impact.
    </p>
  </li>
  <li class="ctdc-partner-path">
    <h3 class="ctdc-partner-path__title">Government</h3>
    <p class="ctdc-partner-path__body">
      We help local government work more effectively — building community
      outreach tools, service request forms, and other technology that improves
      how government interacts with constituents.
    </p>
  </li>
  <li class="ctdc-partner-path">
    <h3 class="ctdc-partner-path__title">Activists &amp; community members</h3>
    <p class="ctdc-partner-path__body">
      Have an idea for how technology could solve a problem in your community?
      Those closest to a problem often know it best. We want to hear from you.
    </p>
  </li>
</ul>
